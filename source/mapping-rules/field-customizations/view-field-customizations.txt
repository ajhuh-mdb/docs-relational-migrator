About this Task
---------------

Steps
-----

#. From the :guilabel:`Mapping` screen, click a table or collection name
   on the :guilabel:`schema model` pane or diagram view.

#. On the :guilabel:`schema model` pane on the right-side of the screen, 
   click the :icon-fa5:`pencil-alt` icon.

#. Click the :icon-fa5:`chevron-left` icon next to a field, any defined
   field customizations are visible in the expanded menu under the field name.
