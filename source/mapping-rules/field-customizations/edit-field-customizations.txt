======================
Edit Customized Fields
======================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 2
   :class: singlecol

You can edit field customized options, you may want to edit a field 
customization for specific indexing or schema requirements.

Steps
-----

#. From the :guilabel:`Mapping` screen, click a table or collection name
   on the :guilabel:`schema model` pane or diagram view.

#. On the :guilabel:`schema model` pane on the right-side of the screen, 
   click the :icon-fa5:`pencil-alt` icon.

#. Click the :icon-fa5:`chevron-left` icon next to the field 
   you want to customize.

#. Edit the field customization, you can:

   - Change a fields :guilabel:`BSON type`.
   - Change a fields :guilabel:`Null handlings` option.
   - Change a fields name.

#. Click :guilabel:`Save and close`.

The updated field customizations are visible in MongoDB after your 
next sync job runs.
